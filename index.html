PASSO

Origin

STORY OF PASSO

PASSO wine bar is born in 1994. The first bar located in Taipei, Taiwan. PASSO is desire to introduce Mise en place Craftsman's spirit into the bar and provide both taste of drinks and decent environment to every guest who stay in PASSO. PASSO believes that every great piece of artwork comes from tiny but precise steps to complete. The spirit of making every steps perfect makes PASSO here to welcome your coming.
